Dear SFLHCC Members:

I am honored to serve as the Chairman of the Board for the South Florida Hispanic Chamber of Commerce (SFLHCC). Our organization has consistently demonstrated a commitment to empowering businesses and professionals throughout South Florida, and it is a privilege to lead such a dynamic and dedicated community.

The SFLHCC has remained steadfast in its mission to support our members through various initiatives, including networking opportunities, educational workshops, advocacy, and access to valuable resources. As we look to the future, I am excited about the opportunities that lie ahead. Our focus will continue to be on fostering economic growth, creating new partnerships, and advocating for policies that benefit our business community. We will also emphasize the importance of innovation and adaptability in today’s rapidly changing business environment.

I want to extend my heartfelt gratitude to each of you for your continued support and involvement. Your participation and engagement are what make our chamber a vibrant and effective organization. Together, we can achieve remarkable success and drive positive change within our community.

Thank you for your dedication and commitment to the South Florida Hispanic Chamber of Commerce. Let us continue to work together towards a prosperous future for all.

Sincerely,
Jose Perez De Corcho
