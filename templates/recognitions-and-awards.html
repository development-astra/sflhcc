From the Chairman

Resilience, growth, and our commitment to you

The South Florida Hispanic Chamber of Commerce is proud to highlight its distinguished recognitions, including the prestigious Kentucky Colonel Award. This award, the highest honor from the Commonwealth of Kentucky, was presented to Liliam M. López, SFLHCC’s President and CEO, in 2022 for her unwavering dedication to community service and over 25 years of impactful leadership. Join us in celebrating this milestone and learn more about the SFLHCC’s commitment to environmental advocacy and the empowerment of the Hispanic and non-Hispanic business community. The Kentucky Colonel Award symbolizes our relentless pursuit of excellence and our mission to inspire positive change. Discover more about our achievements and how we continue to lead the way for businesses.
